Help

This site lets you browse bird observation data submitted to eBird.org. It works worldwide, but the results depend on who's been using it in that region. Don't see any results in your area? Then join eBird and submit your sightings!

Location search field
This is where you enter the location you're interested in. Start typing, then select the location you want from the autocomplete dropdown that appears. Note: you can't select entire countries. For performance reasons, it won't show very large regions: instead, select a city/town or address.
Search options
This is where you choose what results you're interested in. Bird Sightings return all bird sightings in that area. Note: these are only sightings made at locations flagged as "hotspots" within the Ebird system. Sadly, this is currently a limitation with their API. Notable Sightings are sightings flagged as rare or unusual for that location at that date. Popular birding locations lists all locations with reported sightings.
More Search Options
If you click on the "more search options" link, a new section will slide open that provides a little more control over your search. The additional options change depending on your search type.
